The Opus Penthouse

The Opus Penthouse by OMNIYAT is a one-of-a-kind, four-bedroom residence spanning over 12,788 sq. ft. of architectural brilliance. Seamlessly integrated with the iconic design of Zaha Hadid’s masterpiece, this extraordinary space crowns the building at its apex. Expansive views of Dubai and The Opus itself are framed by the sweeping, curvilinear floor-to-ceiling glass curtain wall, where natural light enhances every element of the visionary design.

ME Dubai Hotel

The first and only hotel for which Zaha Hadid designed both the interiors and exteriors. The stylish ME Dubai consists of 100 rooms, complimented by exclusive amenities and dining concepts, masterfully distributed across 250,000 sq.ft. within The Opus. Catering to cultural and fashionable guests, the avant-garde design is further enhanced by the premium service of the ME brand.

INTRODUCING THE OPUS PENTHOUSE BY OMNIYAT

The Opus Penthouse by OMNIYAT is a one-of-one creation within a one of-one masterpiece. Designed and delivered by OMNIYAT Atelier, the four-bedroom penthouse occupies
12,788 sq.ft. of sumptuous living space and units the world’s finest artists, artisans and luxury brands.

Its distinctive floor plan integrates seamlessly with Zaha Hadid’s original creation, while the original trusses and beams have been preserved and exposed, adding a striking feature that pays homage to the building’s structural ingenuity. This is truly a singular masterpiece in the sky, at the epicentre of Dubai.
